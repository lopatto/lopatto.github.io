About

Until recently, I was a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics at Harvard University. I graduated in May and will start a postdoc in the fall. My advisor was Horng-Tzer Yau.

I am interested in probability theory and its applications. My current research interests include random matrix theory and spin glasses.

Before coming to Harvard, I completed my undergraduate education at Washington University in St. Louis.
