About

I am an Assistant Professor and William R. Kenan Jr. Fellow in the Department of Statistics and Operations Research at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

I am interested in probability theory and its applications. My current work focuses on causal inference, random matrix theory, and mathematical physics.
