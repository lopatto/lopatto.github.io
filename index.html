Patrick Lopatto

About Me

I am a Prager Assistant Professor of Applied Mathematics at Brown University.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral member at the Institute for Advanced Study.

I am interested in probability theory and its applications. My work focuses on questions arising from random matrix theory and statistical mechanics.
